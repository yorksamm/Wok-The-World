About Us

Our Mission

Every meal makes a difference. As Asian college students, we often find ourselves missing food from back home and cannot order out due to restaurants not having those dishes or high delivery expenses. Even upon seeing recipes, some are not authentic or still don’t bring a sense of community. The aim of our project, Wok the World, is to build a sense of community by creating a platform where people can share family recipes and those from their culture.
